The Crisis in Core A Village

The 320 families of Core A Village in Lira District, Northern Uganda face a daily crisis that threatens their health, education, and future—lack of clean water.

Currently, women and children walk 3.7 kilometers to collect water from an unprotected, contaminated stream shared with livestock. This dangerous water source is responsible for frequent outbreaks of typhoid, cholera, and diarrheal diseases that claim lives, particularly among young children.

With your support, we can install a borehole well that will transform life in Core A Village forever.
